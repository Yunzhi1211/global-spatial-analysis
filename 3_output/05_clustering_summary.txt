COUNTRY CLUSTERING & PEER GROUP ANALYSIS

METHODOLOGY:
- Algorithm: K-means clustering (k=4)
- Features: log(parks_per_100k), log(population), park_density_score
- Countries analyzed: 96

CLUSTER PROFILES:
  Leaders (High Parks): 16 countries, avg 4.544 parks/100k, top: Denmark, Netherlands, Austria
  Developing (Moderate Parks): 8 countries, avg 3.739 parks/100k, top: Greenland, Luxembourg, Faroe Islands
  Transitioning (Low-Moderate): 31 countries, avg 0.791 parks/100k, top: Lithuania, Norway, New Zealand
  Emerging (Low Parks): 41 countries, avg 0.041 parks/100k, top: Japan, Argentina, Colombia

PEER GROUP STATISTICS:
- Total peer group assignments: 96
- Each country matched to up to 3 closest peers within cluster

KEY FINDINGS:
1. 4 clusters identified with distinct dog park provision patterns
2. Leaders cluster has avg 4.544 parks/100k (16 countries)
3. Emerging cluster has avg 0.041 parks/100k (41 countries)
4. Clear separation between high and low provision countries
5. Regional patterns visible within clusters

APPLICATIONS:
- Peer benchmarking: Countries can compare with similar peers
- Policy transfer: Learn from cluster leaders
- Gap analysis: Identify improvement opportunities within cluster
- Regional cooperation: Cluster membership crosses regional boundaries
