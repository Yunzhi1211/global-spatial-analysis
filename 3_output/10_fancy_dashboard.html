Global Urban Livability Intelligence

 About This Project

This platform provides a comprehensive global analysis of urban livability, integrating data on pet-friendly green spaces, demographic aging trends, urbanization rates, forest cover, and dog park accessibility across 96 countries.

Using OpenStreetMap data, World Bank indicators, and AI-powered clustering, it ranks and compares countries on their ability to provide high-quality urban environments for residents and their pets.

 Data Sources & Indicators

  • Dog Parks: OpenStreetMap (OSM) nodes and ways tagged as dog parks, collected via Overpass API for 96 countries
  • Population: World Bank population estimates (2023)
  • Forest Cover: World Bank forest area as % of land (2023)
  • Urbanization: UN urban population as % of total (2023)
  • Elderly Ratio: World Bank population aged 65+ as % (2023)
